Welcome to my experimental TI4 player dashboard!

Thank you for checking out my little project! The idea here is to provide a simple interface to put in the background on a large TV or monitor so everyone can easily see which player is active and which strategy cards haven't been used. I've also incorporated a simple calculator for the agenda phase. Feel free to poke around!

Features List

  • Multiplayer game creation
  • Current player indicator
  • Speaker indicator
  • Speaker change button
  • Active/used strategy card indicator
